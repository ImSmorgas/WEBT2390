Altoids Story Flavors Ingredients

Peppermint

Altoids Peppermint is the classic flavor that started it all. These iconic mints deliver a powerful burst of peppermint freshness, perfect for freshening your breath anytime, anywhere.

Wintergreen

Altoids Wintergreen combines the bold flavor of wintergreen with the intense freshness Altoids is known for. These mints offer a crisp and minty taste that leaves your mouth feeling cool and revitalized.

Cinnamon

Altoids Cinnamon packs a spicy punch with its fiery cinnamon flavor. These mints are perfect for those who enjoy a little heat in their breath fresheners, providing a warm and invigorating sensation with every mint.

Spearmint

Altoids Spearmint offers a refreshing twist on the classic mint flavor. With its smooth and slightly sweet taste, Spearmint Altoids provide a cool and invigorating sensation that leaves your breath feeling clean and fresh.

Smalls

Altoids Smalls are smaller versions of the classic Altoids mints, perfect for on-the-go freshness. Available in various flavors, including Peppermint and Wintergreen, Altoids Smalls offer the same intense freshness in a convenient, pocket-sized tin.

Arctic Peppermint

Altoids Arctic Peppermint takes the classic peppermint flavor to the next level with an extra-cool sensation. These mints deliver a refreshing blast of peppermint flavor combined with an icy chill, leaving your breath feeling incredibly fresh.

Arctic Strawberry

Altoids Arctic Strawberry offers a sweet and refreshing twist on traditional mint flavors. With a burst of fruity strawberry flavor, these mints provide a delightful alternative for those looking to freshen their breath with a hint of sweetness.

Arctic Wintergreen

Altoids Arctic Wintergreen offers a cool and invigorating experience with its intense wintergreen flavor. These mints provide a refreshing burst of minty goodness that leaves your mouth feeling cool, crisp, and revitalized.
